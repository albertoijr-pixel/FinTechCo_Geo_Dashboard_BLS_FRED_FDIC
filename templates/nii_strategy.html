Methodology & Assumptions  ·  Fed Funds Rate (DFF) is live from FRED (St. Louis Fed), 1996–present.  Market CC Rate is fetched live at page load via web search (Bankrate / FRED series search); historical chart blends TERMCBCCALLNS FRED data (1996–2012) with the live web rate for 2012–present.  CC Delinquency (DRCCLACBS) is a quarterly FRED series; the most recent observation reflects the standard quarterly publication lag (~1 quarter).  NIM = (Market CC Rate × CC Discount) − (Fed Funds + Deposit Premium).  Projected NII = State Total Deposits (FDIC 2023) × Capture Rate × NIM.  This is a strategic planning model, not a financial forecast.  Actual NII depends on credit losses, opex, regulatory capital, and realized deposit/loan mix.
